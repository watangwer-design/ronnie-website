About DIVINE SECONDARY SCHOOL

Excellence through discipline, faith, and community transformation.

Vision
To become the leading provider of education transforming students into self-reliant and esteemed citizens ready to meet the challenges of life.
Mission
To provide quality education that empowers the youths to become self-reliant and job creators.

History: Divine College Buyaga is a beacon of excellence through discipline, faith, and community transformation. Established in 2005, the school has grown to become a pillar of quality education in the Bulambuli district.

Motto: ATTITUDE FOR ALTITUDE — a reminder that the right mindset elevates one to greater heights.
